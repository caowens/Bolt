San Fransisco

Contra Costa Contra Costa Chapter currently consists of seventeen members and approximately twenty-six classic cars. They are family and community oriented sharing a common interest in owning, maintaining and restoring quality classic cars, that range from the mid 1930's thru 1959. We plan social events, meet monthly and travel together as a club to various car shows. The Club hosts several events, one is the Annual Toy Drive each November, which benefits a local charity. We contribute to events that give back to local community.
